Confidential Report

Date: 15.09.2020
Location: Katowice, Poland

Subject: Ewa Nowak

Incident Details:
On 15.09.2020, an operation codenamed "Sweet Surrender" was conducted by our undercover agents in response to reports of illegal sweets distribution within the city of Katowice. The operation's primary objective was to apprehend the key individual behind this covert confectionery operation.

Investigation Findings:
Our investigation revealed that Ewa Nowak had been operating under multiple aliases to disguise her involvement in the illegal sweets trade. Surveillance and intelligence efforts indicated her central role in orchestrating the distribution network, implicating her as the mastermind behind the operation.

Apprehension:
On 15.09.2020, law enforcement agents successfully located and apprehended Ewa Nowak at an undisclosed location in Katowice. The suspect was taken into custody without incident.

Seizure of Evidence:
During the apprehension, a significant quantity of illicit sweets was discovered and seized as evidence from the premises. These sweets are believed to be connected to the illegal trade.

Legal Action:
Ewa Nowak is currently in custody and will face charges related to her involvement in the illegal distribution of sweets. Legal proceedings will be initiated to bring her to justice and dismantle the wider network.

Conclusion:
The "Sweet Surrender" operation marks a significant milestone in our efforts to combat illegal confectionery distribution in Katowice. Ewa Nowak's arrest sends a clear message that such illicit activities will not be tolerated within our community.

This report is classified as confidential and is for authorized personnel only.


KOMENDA MIEJSKA POLICJI W KATOWICACH
