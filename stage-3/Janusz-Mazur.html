Confidential Report

Date: 26.11.2021
Location: Lublin, Poland

Subject: Janusz Mazur

Incident Details:
On 26.11.2021, an operation codenamed "Candy Caper" was conducted by our undercover agents in response to reports of illegal confectionery distribution within the city of Lublin. The operation's primary objective was to apprehend the key individual behind this covert confectionery operation.

Investigation Findings:
Our investigation revealed that Janusz Mazur had been operating under multiple aliases to disguise his involvement in the illegal confectionery trade. Surveillance and intelligence efforts indicated his central role in orchestrating the distribution network, implicating him as the mastermind behind the operation.

Apprehension:
On 26.11.2021, law enforcement agents successfully located and apprehended Janusz Mazur at an undisclosed location in Lublin. The suspect was taken into custody without incident.

Seizure of Evidence:
During the apprehension, a significant quantity of illicit confectionery was discovered and seized as evidence from the premises. These confectioneries are believed to be connected to the illegal trade.

Legal Action:
Janusz Mazur is currently in custody and will face charges related to his involvement in the illegal distribution of confectionery. Legal proceedings will be initiated to bring him to justice and dismantle the wider network.

Conclusion:
The "Candy Caper" operation marks a significant milestone in our efforts to combat illegal confectionery distribution in Lublin. Janusz Mazur's arrest sends a clear message that such illicit activities will not be tolerated within our community.

This report is classified as confidential and is for authorized personnel only.


KOMENDA MIEJSKA POLICJI W LUBLINIE
