Come down the rabbit hole

As a college student, you spend most of your time surfing the internet and playing online games. One day, while browsing the web, you come across a file that piques your interest. The file seems to be encrypted, and you find yourself trying to crack the code. After hours of trying, you finally unlock the file, and to your shock, it contains detailed information about a criminal syndicate involved in trafficking narcotics into some of the European countries. You realize the danger of the situation and decide to take matters into your own hands.
